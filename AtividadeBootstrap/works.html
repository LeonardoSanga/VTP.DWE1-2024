Written and directed by Gary Dauberman, and based on Stephen King‘s 1975 novel ‘Salem’s Lot, the film stars Lewis Pullman, Makenzie Leigh, Bill Camp, Pilou Asbæk, Alfre Woodard, and William Sadler.
